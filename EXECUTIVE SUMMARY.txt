Project Title:
Unlocking Revenue: Identifying Bottlenecks in Regional Sales Pipeline


Prepared By:
Shrey Patel
--------------------------------------------------------------
PROJECT OVERVIEW
--------------------------------------------------------------


This project analyzes the RegionalSales2025 dataset consisting of
10,000 retail sales transactions recorded during the year 2025.
The objective of the analysis was to identify revenue bottlenecks,
sales trends, regional performance, product performance, customer
return behavior, and sales agent effectiveness.


MySQL was used to perform data analysis through SQL queries, while
Microsoft Excel was used to create an interactive dashboard using
Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, KPI Cards, Slicers, and Conditional
Formatting.


The findings from this analysis provide useful insights that can
help management improve business performance and make informed
decisions.


--------------------------------------------------------------
OBJECTIVES
--------------------------------------------------------------


• Analyze monthly sales trends.
• Identify regions with the highest revenue and revenue loss.
• Calculate cancellation and return percentages.
• Determine top-performing sales agents.
• Measure sales contribution by product category.
• Identify products causing maximum revenue loss.
• Detect customers with frequent product returns.
• Build an interactive dashboard for business reporting.


--------------------------------------------------------------
KEY FINDINGS
--------------------------------------------------------------


1. Monthly Sales Analysis
   Monthly sales remained relatively stable throughout the year,
   with some months showing higher revenue due to seasonal demand.
   Understanding these trends helps management prepare inventory
   and marketing campaigns more effectively.


2. Regional Performance
   Sales varied across different regions. Some regions generated
   higher revenue, while others experienced more cancelled and
   returned orders. These regions require additional attention to
   improve operational efficiency.


3. Product Category Performance
   Electronics and Home Appliances contributed a significant
   portion of the total revenue. Categories with lower sales
   present opportunities for promotional campaigns and product
   improvements.


4. Revenue Loss Analysis
   Cancelled and returned orders resulted in noticeable revenue
   loss. A small number of products contributed disproportionately
   to these losses, indicating potential quality or customer
   satisfaction issues.


5. Sales Agent Performance
   Several sales agents consistently achieved higher sales than
   others. Their sales strategies and customer engagement methods
   could be shared across the sales team to improve overall
   performance.


6. Customer Return Analysis
   A small group of customers returned products multiple times.
   This behavior may indicate product defects, customer
   dissatisfaction, or misuse and should be investigated further.


--------------------------------------------------------------
BUSINESS RECOMMENDATIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------


• Improve inventory management to reduce order cancellations.


• Review products with high return rates and improve quality
  control processes.


• Reward and recognize high-performing sales agents through
  incentive programs.


• Increase marketing efforts in lower-performing regions to
  improve revenue.


• Monitor customer return patterns regularly to identify
  fraudulent or unusual purchasing behavior.


• Continue tracking monthly sales trends for better demand
  forecasting and inventory planning.

--------------------------------------------------------------
CONCLUSION
--------------------------------------------------------------


The Regional Sales analysis successfully identified important
business trends, sales opportunities, and operational bottlenecks.

The recommendations provided in this report can help improve
